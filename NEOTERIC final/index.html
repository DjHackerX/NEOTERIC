What is IEDC?

Where student ideas turn into ventures.

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) is a Kerala Startup Mission initiative that lives inside our campus — a space where students move from curiosity to creation. NEOTERIC is our chapter: a community of builders who prototype, pitch, and launch before they graduate.

We believe innovation isn't a subject you study — it's a habit you practice. Through mentorship, funding pathways, and hands-on labs, IEDC gives every student a real shot at building something that matters.
